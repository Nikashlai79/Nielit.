NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

NIELIT Imphal, founded in 1989, is a premier technical institute under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. Located in Akampat, 5 km from Imphal city, it offers formal courses like Diploma in ECE and CSE, BCA, MCA, and MSc IT, affiliated with Manipur University. Non-formal courses include NIELIT ‘O’ Level and certifications in AI, Big Data, and Office Automation. With modern facilities, NIELIT Imphal promotes digital literacy and offers scholarships to SC/ST/PH/female students.

The campus features smart classrooms, high-speed internet labs, a digital library, seminar halls, hostels, and a cafeteria. Admissions to formal courses are based on Manipur University criteria, while non-formal courses accept direct applications. Courses include Diploma in ECE/CSE, BCA, MCA, MSc IT, and certifications in AI, Cyber Security, and more, equipping students for the tech industry.
